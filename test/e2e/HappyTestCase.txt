### Happy test case

User creates a new Idea
This idea gets some likes.
Idea reaches quorum.
Creator of idea starts a new poll.

A second idea is created, gets likes and reaches its quorum.
This second idea is merged into the above poll as alternative proposal.

Some more ideas are also merged into the poll.)

(Proposals can still be edited in the elaboration phase.)

The creator of the initial proposal starts the voting phase.

(Proposals in this poll cannot be edited anymore.)

Users cast their votes.

One proposal wins.
